# Privacy Policy — Calorie Fasting Tracker

**Last updated: June 27, 2026**

## Summary
Calorie Fasting Tracker stores all your data on your device. We do not collect, 
transmit, or share your personal information.

## Data We Store
All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:
- Food entries (name, calories, macros, serving size, date, meal type)
- Meal photos (stored on-device only)
- Daily nutrition goals
- Fasting window history
- Subscription status (cached locally via UserDefaults)

## Data We Do Not Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools
- We do not track your location
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not have servers or a backend — there is no user account

## Camera and Photos
The app requests camera and photo library access solely to let you attach 
photos to food log entries. Photos are stored on your device only and never 
transmitted.

## Network Requests
The app makes network requests only for:
- **Barcode scanning**: product nutrition data is fetched from 
  [Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org), a public open-source 
  food database. No personal data is sent — only the barcode number.
- **USDA food search**: uses a bundled local database — no network request.
- **Subscription purchases**: handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. 
  We never see your payment information.

## Subscriptions
Subscription purchases and management are handled by Apple. We receive only 
a verified transaction confirmation. See Apple's Privacy Policy at 
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy for details on how Apple handles 
payment data.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect 
data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes 
acceptance. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
