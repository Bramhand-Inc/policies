## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is an account required?**
No. MacroFast requires no account, no sign-in, and no registration. Everything is stored privately on your device.

**Where is my data stored?**
All data — food entries, macro history, goals, and fasting records — is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's encrypted on-device storage. Nothing is sent to any server.

**What data sources are used for food search?**
Food search uses the USDA FoodData Central database, which is bundled directly in the app — no internet connection needed for search. Barcode lookups use the Open Food Facts API, an open-source, community-maintained database. Barcode requests are anonymous and contain no personal information.

**My fasting timer seems off — why?**
MacroFast calculates your fasting window automatically from your last logged meal. If the timer looks wrong, check whether the last entry was logged at the correct time, or whether it was an item marked "exclude from fast" (such as black coffee or water).

**How do I exclude a food from my fast?**
Long press any food entry → Edit → toggle "Exclude from fast." Zero-calorie, zero-macro items like black coffee are excluded automatically.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → MacroFast**. You can cancel anytime. Access to Premium features continues until the end of the current billing period.

**I purchased Premium but the app still shows the paywall.**
Tap "Restore Purchase" on the paywall screen. If the issue persists, sign out and back into your Apple ID, then try restoring again.

**Can I transfer my data to a new iPhone?**
Yes — iCloud backup includes your MacroFast data if you have iCloud Backup enabled. Restore your new iPhone from an iCloud backup and your history will carry over.
